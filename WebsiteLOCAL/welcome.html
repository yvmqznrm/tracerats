welcome to tracerats!

tracerats: uprising of the data points is a video game about hackers that combines CTF-like challenges with a story / roleplaying aspect that reflects some realities of actual hackers, including mental health issues resulting from hacktivism.

it aims to engage people in learning more about the hacker scene and hacktivism, the technology behind it, and how it affects the lives of the people involved with it, thereby raising awareness and encouraging them to get involved.
